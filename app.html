About the Features

The model uses 8 clinical variables drawn directly from the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset.

🩸

Glucose

Plasma glucose concentration from a 2-hour oral glucose tolerance test. The single most predictive feature.

Highest Impact
⚖️

BMI

Body mass index — weight in kg divided by the square of height in metres. Strong predictor of metabolic risk.

High Impact
🧬

Diabetes Pedigree

A function that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history and genetic inheritance.

Genetic Factor
🎂

Age

Risk increases with age. Patients in this dataset range from 21 to 81 years old.

Demographic
💧

Blood Pressure

Diastolic blood pressure in mmHg. Hypertension is often comorbid with type 2 diabetes.

Moderate Impact
💉

Insulin

2-hour serum insulin level. Elevated values may indicate insulin resistance.

Metabolic
